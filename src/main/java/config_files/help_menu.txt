THE PURPOSE OF THIS GAME IS TO
KILL ALL THE INVADING MOSTERS
AND KEEP YOUR FORTRESS INTACT.
RIGHT CLICK ON TOWER ICONS TO
PLACE TOWERS ON THE MAP.
LEFT CLICK ON THE UPGRADE BUTTON AND
THEN LEFT CLICK ONCE AGAIN ON THE CHOSEN TOWER
TO UPGRADE IT.
SAME PROCESS WITH THE LEFT BUTTON.
DOUBLE CLICK ON A TOWER TO SEE IT'S SPECIFICATIONS AND RANGE.
DOUBLE CLICK AGAIN ON IT TO DISABLE THE FORMER ACTION EFFECT.
